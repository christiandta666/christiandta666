## 🚀 About Me

I am a QA Engineer with around 5 years of hands-on experience working across different types of projects, mainly focused on functional testing, API validation, and integrations.

I have worked closely with cross-functional teams testing new features end-to-end, from UI validation to backend behavior. My day-to-day work includes designing test scenarios, identifying edge cases, and making sure releases are stable and reliable. I also have experience testing APIs using Postman and working with automation tools like Cypress, Playwright, and Karate.

On the backend side, I have been involved in integration testing, validating data flows between systems, reviewing logs in Amazon CloudWatch, and troubleshooting issues related to webhooks and external services. I have also worked with automation and integrations using Workato, where understanding how systems communicate is key.

I have also had experience as a Salesforce QA, testing workflows, validations, and integrations within the platform to ensure business processes run correctly.

I enjoy digging into issues, understanding root causes, and providing clear, useful feedback to developers. At the same time, I understand priorities and know when to go deep and when to keep things moving.

Recently, I have been focusing on improving my automation skills and expanding my knowledge in full stack development to become a more well-rounded QA Engineer.
